Insider Tips

🕐 Timing

Book Bar Eugenie, Bar Isabel, and No Vacancy at least 2 weeks out. Walk-in at the bar is easier than a table. For St. Lawrence Market, arrive before 10am Saturday for the best selection.

🚇 Getting Around

TTC streetcar on Queen West for bars and cafés. Ferry from Jack Layton Terminal for the Islands — book online on summer weekends. Scarborough Bluffs is easiest by car or rideshare.

🌤️ Best Season

May–June is peak Toronto — warm enough to be outside, not yet too crowded, and the terraces are just opening up. Late April for High Park cherry blossoms.

💸 Free Days

AGO is pay-what-you-can on Wednesday evenings. Power Plant is free Fridays. High Park, ravines, Trinity Bellwoods, Harbourfront boardwalk — all completely free.
